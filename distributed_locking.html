Architectural Trade-offs

Comparing different locking strategies based on Safety vs. Performance.

Redlock (Redis)

Relies on clocks. High performance, but unsafe for critical data integrity (CP vs AP).

Consensus (ZK/Etcd)

Safety first. Handles partitions correctly via ephemeral nodes. Higher latency.

Fencing Tokens

The ultimate shield. Requires storage layer support (atomic compare). Prevents zombie writes.
